ERA BATTLE

Evolve through the ages & crush the enemy stronghold

  • Spawn Units: Spend Gold to recruit units. They march right and attack automatically. Gold also trickles in over time.
  • Earn Gold & XP: Defeating enemy forces rewards currency and experience.
  • Evolve Era: Bank enough XP to advance Stone → Ancient → Medieval → Modern → Future. Each era unlocks stronger units, towers and a new special.
  • Defend: Unlock slots and build towers on your base to repel attackers.
  • Special Attacks: Fire your era's signature weapon (40s cooldown) to clear a swarm.
  • Camera: Drag to pan, scroll to zoom, hit FIT (or 0) to see the whole field. Hotkeys: 1/2/3 spawn, E evolve, Space special.
  • Online: One player creates a match and shares the 4-letter code; the other joins. You always command the blue army on the left.
