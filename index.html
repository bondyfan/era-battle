A classic 2D base-defense evolution war game

How to Play

  • Spawn Units: Use Gold to recruit units. They automatically march right to destroy the enemy base.
  • Earn Gold & XP: Defeating enemy forces rewards you with currency and experience.
  • Evolve Era: Accumulate enough XP to advance to the next age (Stone → Ancient → Medieval → Modern → Future). Evolution unlocks stronger base designs, new towers, new special attacks, and modern units!
  • Defend: Build defensive towers on your base to ward off invading units.
  • Special Attacks: Use your era's unique weapon with a cooldown to clear enemy groups when overwhelmed.
  • Controls: Drag the battlefield canvas to scroll or hover near edges. Use keyboard hotkeys 1, 2, 3 to spawn units, E to evolve, and Space for the special attack.
