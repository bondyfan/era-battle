ERA BATTLE

Evolve through the ages & crush the enemy stronghold

  • Spawn & Send Units: Spend Gold to recruit units in this top-down view, then DRAG a unit onto a lane to send it there. It marches toward the enemy and attacks automatically. Gold also trickles in over time.
  • Pick a Lane: The MID lane is free; unlock the TOP and BOTTOM lanes for gold to open extra attack routes.
  • Earn Gold & XP: Defeating enemy forces rewards currency and experience.
  • Evolve Era: Bank enough XP to advance Stone → Ancient → Medieval → Modern → Future. Each era unlocks stronger units, towers and a new special.
  • Defend: Unlock slots and build towers — you can only place them on YOUR half of the field — to repel attackers.
  • Special Attacks: Buy your special, then upgrade it through 4 levels (Lv1 chips away, Lv4 is brutal). Fire it on a 40s cooldown to wipe a swarm.
  • Range Upgrades: Pay to extend your ranged units' reach so they hit the enemy deeper in the line before it closes in.
  • Camera: Drag the battlefield to move the orbit camera around the field. Hotkeys: 1/2/3 spawn, E evolve, Space special.
  • Online: One player creates a match and shares the 4-letter code; the other joins. You always command the blue army on the left.
